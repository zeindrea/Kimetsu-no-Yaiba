Breath of the Thunder

The Breath of Thunder (雷かみなりの呼こ吸きゅう Kaminari no kokyū?), or Breath of Lightning in some publications, is one of the five main Breath Styles directly derived from the Breath of the Sun. The primary Cultivator for the Demon Slaying Corps was Jigoro Kuwajima.

Attacks

There are a total of six original styles that can be used with the Breath of Thunder. However, Zenitsu Agatsuma developed a seventh style in order to stand on equal ground with Kaigaku, his former senior disciple under Jigoro.

  • First Style: Thunderclap and Flash (壱いちノ型かた 霹へき靂れき一いっ閃せん Ichi no kata: Hekireki Issen?)[1] - The user dashes forward and slashes at their opponent with incredible speed. Zenitsu has honed this ability to the extent of being able to repeat the dash multiple times in quick succession.
    • Six Fold (六ろく連れん Rokuren?)[2] - A compilation of the First Style with a series of six dashes. Primarily used by Zenitsu.
    • Eight Fold (八はち連れん Hachiren?)[3] - Zenitsu dashes eight times within one use of this technique.
    • God Speed (神しん速そく Shinsoku?) - Further augments the speed of Thunderclap and Flash.
  • Second Style: Lightning Ball (弐にノ型かた 稲いな魂だま Ni no kata: Inadama?)[4] - The swordsman is able to generate a series of five straight thunder attacks all performed in a single moment.
  • Third Style: Buzzing Mosquito Thunder (参さんノ型かた 聚しう蚊ぶん成せい雷らい San no kata: Shiubun seirai?)[5] - Generates a spinning wave of lightning in all directions.
  • Fourth Style: Distant Thunder (肆しノ型かた 遠えん雷らい Shi no kata: Enrai?)[6] - A ranged thunder strike.
  • Fifth Style: Heat Lightning (伍ごノ型かた 熱れつ界かい雷らい Go no kata: Retsu kairai?)[7] - A slashing attack composed of lightning that cracks skin and burns flesh.
  • Sixth Style: Electric Lightning Shower (陸ろくノ型かた 電でん轟ごう雷らい轟ごう Roku no kata:Dengou raigou?)[8] - A series of thunder strikes that cracks the target's body.
