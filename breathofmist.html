Breath of Mist

Breath of Mist Style (霞かすみの呼こ吸きゅう Kasumi no kokyū?) It is a breath style derived from the Breath of Wind. The Cultivator for the Demon Slaying Corps is not specified. However, it is introduced as Muichiro's primary skill developed through his intense training.

Attacks
  • First Form: Hanging Sky, Distant Mist (壱いちノ型かた 垂すい天てん遠とお霞がすみ Ichi no kata: Suiten Tōgasumi?)- A straightforward thrust attack.
  • Second Form: Eight Layered Mist (弐にノ型かた 八や重え霞かすみ Ni no kata: Yaekasumi?)[1]- Muichiro unleashes multiple slashes one on top of the other in quick succession.
  • Third Form: Demisting Blast (参さんノ型かた 霞か散さんの飛し沫ぶき San no kata: Kasan no Shibuki?)- A circular slash that blows away projectile attacks from enemies.
  • Fourth Form: Advection Slash (肆しノ型かた 移い流りゅう斬きり Shi no kata: Iryūkiri?) - A quick slash from the sheathe reminiscent of an Iaidō technique.
  • Fifth Form: Sea of Clouds and Haze (伍ごノ型かた 霞か雲うんの海うみ Go no kata: Kaun no Umi?)[2] - Muichiro charges towards an enemy attack at high speed and unleashes a flurry of slashes.
  • Six Form: Moonlit Mist (陸ろくノ型かた 月つきの霞か 消しょう Roku no kata: Tsuki no Kashō?)- Muichiro leaps high in the air and sends a series of countless ranged slashes downwards.
  • Seventh form: Haze (漆さちノ型かた 朧おぼろ Sachi no kata: Oboro?) - A technique in which Muichiro drastically changes the tempo of his movement to disorient an enemy. When he shows himself, his speed will appear to be extremely slow but when he hides, he moves in the blink of an eye.This Technique was created by Muichiro Tokito during his battle against Upper Moon Five, Gyokko.-
