Breath of Wind

Breath of Wind (風かぜの呼こ吸きゅう Kaze no Kokyū?) is one of the five Breath Styles derived directly from the Breath of the Sun. The Breath of the Beast, despite being independently developed by Inosuke Hashibira, is a derivative of the Breath of Wind, so it can be assumed that both styles use an enhanced sense of touch in their techniques.

Attacks
  • First Style: Dust Whirlwind Cutter (壱いちノ型かた 鹿じん旋せん風ぷう・削そぎ Ichi no kata: Jin Senpū・Sogi?) - The swordsman dashes forward in a cyclone of slashes.
  • Second Style: Claws Purifying Wind (弐にノ型かた 爪そう々そう・科しな戸と風かぜ Ni no kata: Sōsō・Shinato Kaze ?)[1] - The swordsman lifts the sword upwards towards the right, above his head and does a vertical slash down creating four blades of wind that slashes down on the enemy like a claws.
  • Third Style: Clean Storm Wind Tree (参さんノ型かた 晴せい嵐らん風ふう樹じゅ San no kata: Seiran Fūju ?) - The swordsman uses his arm to slash around his body to create a whirlwind around him that slices up everything in its path.
  • Fourth Style: Rising Dust Storm (肆しノ型かた 昇しょう上じょう砂さ塵じん嵐らん Shi no kata: Shōjō Sajinran?) - The swordsman swings his blade downwards from several directions.
  • Fifth Style: Winter Gale (伍ごノ型かた 木こ枯がらし颪おろし Go no kata: Kogarashi Oroshi?) - The swordsman strikes from above while falling in a multitude of arced slashes.
  • Sixth Style: Black Smoke Storm (陸ろくノ型かた 黒こく風ふう烟えん嵐らん Roku no kata: Kokufū Enran?)[2] - The swordsman strikes from the side, while rotating the body in a position to deliver a uppercut movement, creating a slashing tornado.
