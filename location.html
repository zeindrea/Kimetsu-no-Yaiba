Location

Sagiri Mountain

Sagiri Mountain is a huge mountain in a mostly unpopulated region. The air is very thin near its peak, making it very difficult to breathe.

History

The mountain is used by Urokodaki who uses the mountain to train children who are craving to become a Demon Slayer. Urokodaki tests his students by tasking them by slicing a bolder using a katana and if they succeded then they are sent to participate in the Final Selection. Children who complete the Final Selection are accepted as Demon Slayers. For those who die in the Final Selection, their spirits return to the mountain.

Natagumo Mountain

Natagumo Mountain (那田蜘蛛山) is a mountain where the majority of it is covered with thick trees. The entire place has plenty of spider webs scattered around. The mountain serves as a home to the Spider Family.

History

The family wants to live in peace and whenever an intruder approaches the mountain, the Mother Spider Demon uses her threads to control the intruders and then kills them. The mountain is ruled by Rui, a lower five demon, he is also the head of the Spider Family. Rui makes sure that all of his family members are doing their task to keep them safe.

Butterfly Estate

The Butterly Estate is the manor that the Insect Pillar, Shinobu Kocho, and Kanao Tsuyuri live in. The manor is often used as a recovery base for injured demon slayers. The majority of healing is done by Shinobu, though there are also attendants known as Kakushi who run errands and assist in watching over the injured. On top of this, there are others who live in the estate that are not demon slayers, but offer training regiments to facilitate the recovery process and help the injured demon slayers reach top condition again. For example, Aoi created a training regiment meant to help Tanjiro, Inosuke, and Zenitsu recover after their battles on Mount Natagumo.
