Breath of the Flames

Breath of Flames (炎ほのおの呼こ吸きゅう Honō no kokyū?) is one of the five main Breath Styles directly derived from the Breath of the Sun.

Attacks
  • First Style: Sea of Fire (壱いちノ型かた 不知火しらぬい Ichi no kata: Shiranui?)[1] - The swordsman charges towards his opponent at high speed and decapitates them in one or multiple slashes.
  • Second Style: Ascending Blazing Sky (弐にノ型かた 昇のぼり炎えん天てん Ni no kata: Nobori en ten?)[2]- The swordsman uses an upwards moving slash attack.
  • Third Style: Not yet shown.
  • Fourth Style: Wave of Blazing Flames (肆しノ型かた 盛せい炎えんのうねり Shi no kata: Sei en no uneri?)[3] - The swordsman spins his sword in a circular motion, deflecting his enemies attacks.
  • Fifth Style: Flame Tiger (伍ごノ型かた 炎えん虎こ Go no kata: Enko?)[4] - A series on sword slashes that take on the form of a tiger.
  • Sixth Style: Not yet shown.
  • Seventh Style: Not yet shown.
  • Eighth Style: Not yet shown.
  • Esoterica (奥おう義ぎ Ōgi?): Ninth Style: Purgatory (玖くノ型かた 煉れん獄ごく Ku no kata: Rengoku?)[5] - A devastating dashing slash initiated from a high stance. The technique carves a deep impression in the ground.
