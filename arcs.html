Story Arcs

Final Selection Arc

The Final Selection Arc is the first arc of Kimetsu no Yaiba. This arc focuses on Tanjiro's next step after his family was slaughtered and the lone survivor, his sister Nezuko, turned into a demon. He must now train in order to pass the Final Selection.

Sypnosis

Tanjiro seeks out Sakonji Urokodaki and undergoes training to become a demon slayer. While preparing to partake in the Final Selection, an event when individuals are left to fend off demons for seven nights, he encounters Sabito and Makomo who help train him. At the selection, Tanjiro encounters a mysterious demon with a grudge against Urokodaki and discovers there's a long backstory between the two then what he would've originally expected.

Pillar Training Arc

The Pillar Training Arc is the tenth arc of Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Sypnosis

The Pillar Training conducted by the pillars is a special training session that aims to improve all demon slayers' abilities and physical health. The training is split between the pillars. First, the Sound Pillar Uzui Tengen conducts training on basic stamina. Then the Love Pillar Kanroji Mitsuri handles flexibility training and the Mist Pillar Tokitou Muichirou deals with the quick movement training.

The Snake Pillar, Obanai Iguro, then focuses on sword skill revision, followed up by the Wind Pillar Sanemi Shinazugawa who handles pounding training and finally the Rock Pillar Gyomei Himejima who trains muscle reinforcement. The Insect Pillar Shinobu Kocho does not take part in the training.

The majority of the demon slayers view the training session as an everlasting trip in hell, with the exception of Tanjirou Kamado and Inosuke Hashibira who see the session as a chance to improve themselves. As a general rule, pillars must take part in each other's training sessions. It is considered a win-win situation for both the pillars and the demon slayers as even the pillars get to improve due to being constantly attacked by wave upon wave of juniors ranked lower than themselves.
