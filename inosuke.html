Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke Hashibira

Inosuke Hashibira (嘴はし平びら伊い之の助すけ Hashibira Inosuke?) is a Kanoe ranked Demon Slayer and member of the Demon Slaying Corps and a traveling companion of Tanjirou Kamado.

Appearance

Inosuke is usually seen wearing a grey boar mask, with a shirtless version of the Demon Slayer uniform. Without the mask, he has messy shoulder-length black hair that fades to a blue color, and large green eyes with long eyelashes. He is described as having a girly looking face which significantly contrasts with his muscular body.

Personality

Inosuke is extraordinarily short-tempered and proud and makes a big deal out of fighting opponents stronger than him claiming that's his only hobby.[1] Inosuke will not think about the difference in power between him and his opponent; this often led him to be in danger. He is a sore loser and is always trying to compete with Tanjiro and provoke him into fighting with him and usually failing. Due to growing up in the mountains by himself Inosuke has trouble interacting with others and only cares about himself most of the time. However, as time goes by, Inosuke becomes more influenced by Tanjiro, and he also starts to think and to use strategies before going head-on with an opponent.

Abilities and Power
Natural Abilities

Enhanced sense of touch: After growing up in the mountains, Inosuke developed a sharp sense of touch. He can even feel small vibrations in the air.

Flexibility: Inosuke has an extremely strong sense of flexibility, to the point where he is able to dislocate any of the joints in his body by his own will.

Swordsmanship

Breath of the Beast (獣ケダモノの呼こ吸きゅう Kedamono no kokyū?): Developed by Inosuke after living in the mountains. With his enhanced sense of touch, he can utilize several different techniques.

  • First Fang: Pierce and Extract (壱いちノ牙きば 穿うがち抜ぬき Ichi no kiba: Ugachi nuki?): Inosuke stabs the target's neck with both blades.
  • Second Fang: Rip and Tear (弐にノ牙きば 切きり裂さき Ni no kiba: Kirisaki?): A double slash with his two blades in both diagonals (like X)
  • Third Fang: Rip and Devour (参さんノ牙きば 喰くい裂ざき San no kiba: Kuizaki?): Simultaneous horizontal slashes towards the target's throat.
  • Fourth Fang: Mince to Bits (肆しノ牙きば 切きり細こま裂ざき Shi no kiba: Kiri koma zaki?): A multiple diagonal double slashes with both swords.
